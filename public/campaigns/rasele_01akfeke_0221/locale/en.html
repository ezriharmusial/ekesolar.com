RURAL_AFRICA SOLAR ELECTRIFICATION PROJECTS (RASELE PROJECTS 2021)

Date: 12-02-2021
Ref: ra/s/ele_01/akf/eke_0221

The return of the Sun 2021 means light for those in darkness. Physically and Spiritually speaking, the Continent of Africa which has been in the dark for decades is now ready and set to receive light from the Almighty Sun.

Thanks to ArtKids Foundation Holland and EkeSolar Africa Unlimited for their comprehensive sensitization of the African people on the immeasurable ability of the Sun's Energy to forever solve Africa's Electrification challenges.We are grateful.

To continue these important "Rural_Africa Electrification projects", ArtKids Foundation Holland is inviting Solar Energy Manufacturers, Experts,Companies, Philanthropists, Donors and Lovers of Africa to contribute Solar Energy products and funds (CSR) with which the ongoing Rural_Africa Solar Electrification projects are to be sustained and achieved.

Please contact us today for a joint venture partnership, donations or grants.

Welcome to "illuminated Africa" 2021+.
